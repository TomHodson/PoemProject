As part of Imperial College London's poet-in-residence outreach program, poet Dan Simpson has collaborated with Tom Hodson, a PhD student in physics. Using Tom's research into collective behaviour and unconventional states of matter as inspiration, Dan and Tom collaborated to create poetry on the themes of chaos, order and heat. They chose to give the pieces a digital format with changing visual elements to reinforce the science behind each text. Produced with creative computing student Sophie Nadel, these are the resulting poetry experiments.

Tom Hodson


Tom is a PhD student at Imperial College London looking at how electrons move through disordered crystal lattices. He spends his time writing code, doing mathematics
